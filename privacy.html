Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 19, 2025

Expensei respects your privacy. We do not sell your personal data and only collect what is necessary to run the service.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address and basic profile/preferences.
  • App data: expense entries and optional receipt images you upload (kept private to your account).
  • Technical data: device info and basic analytics/crash data to improve stability and performance.

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide core features (recording expenses, insights, backups).
  • Improve the app (bug fixes, performance, UX).
  • Communicate important updates related to your account or subscription.

Payments

  • Stripe (web checkout): If you subscribe on our website, payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Apple In-App Purchases (IAP): If you purchase a subscription through the App Store, billing is handled directly by Apple. We do not access or store your Apple payment details.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – authentication and secure data storage.
  • Stripe – payment processing for web purchases.
  • Apple App Store (IAP) – billing for in-app purchases on iOS.

Data Retention & Control

  • You can delete expense data or request account deletion at any time; associated data will be removed from our active systems.
  • We retain only what is necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children

Expensei is intended for general audiences. If we learn that we’ve collected personal information from a child without appropriate consent, we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted by updating the effective date and, when appropriate, by additional notice.
